## 1. Descrição Curta (Hunter/Maker description)
Talkvex is an AI-powered goal management tool that transforms long-term ambitions into consistent daily actions. It doesn't just track tasks; it plans, mentors, and adjusts your route weekly based on your real performance.

## 2. Descrição Completa
Most productivity apps are just glorified shopping lists. They tell you *what* to do, but they don't help you *how* or *when* to do it, especially when life gets in the way.

Talkvex closes the gap between the "Big Goal" and the "Daily To-do".

**How it works:**
1. **Define your Destination:** Tell the AI what you want to achieve in natural language.
2. **AI Plan Generator:** Instantly get a 12-month roadmap, quarterly milestones, and your checklist for the first week.
3. **Weekly Review:** Every Monday, your AI mentor chats with you to see what worked and recalibrates the next steps. No guilt, just smart adjustments.
4. **Streak System:** Build momentum with a gamified experience that rewards consistency over intensity.

Stop planning. Start evolving.
