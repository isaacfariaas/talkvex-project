# Talkvex Alpha: Day 1 Recap and Learnings

Yesterday marked the official Alpha launch of Talkvex. Our mission has always been clear: to bridge the gap between long-term ambitions and daily execution using the power of AI.

The response surpassed our most optimistic projections.

## By the Numbers
- **Total Registrations:** 1,247
- **Product Hunt Rank:** Top 10 (and climbing!)
- **Waitlist Conversion:** 85%

## Initial Feedback
Our first cohort of users has been vocal about what they value most:
- **Simplicity:** Turning a goal like "Run a Marathon" into "Buy shoes today" and "Walk 15 mins tomorrow" is a game-changer.
- **Weekly Adjustments:** The AI mentor that doesn't judge, but adjusts the plan when life happens, is the core of the Talkvex experience.

## What's Next?
We are monitoring support tickets closely (shoutout to our DEV team for the quick fixes today!). The next step is introducing **Goal Templates** based on the most requested categories: Career Pivot, Language Learning, and Health/Fitness.

Thank you for being part of this journey.
